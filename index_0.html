Self-Drive and Privately Guided Travel

Immerse yourself in the wild natural beauty of New Zealand, spend time with the locals, and experience the foods, traditions, and the culture of our unique country. An extraordinary travel destination, New Zealand offers stunning scenery, distinctive wildlife, and excellent hiking, biking, adventure, and water activities. Our Maori culture is truly unique. Our food, wine, and beer are world class and exquisite. Arts and cultural opportunities abound as do opportunities for relaxation and pampering. Come and experience ‘middle earth’ for yourself.

More than a ‘trip of a lifetime’, we’ll ensure your journey to New Zealand is unforgettable, filled with transformative experiences.

Specializing in customized travel arrangements, we design your journey based on your interests and travel preferences. We will design an itinerary for you to self-drive or we will happily guide you around New Zealand.
