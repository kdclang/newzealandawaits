Bike, Hike, and Yak I
January 10 - January 24, 2016

If you love seeing the scenery from the saddle of a bike, while hiking up to an alpine meadow, or while gliding along in a kayak, then sign up now! Designed for those who like to be active, this trip requires an average fitness level to enjoy all it has to offer. Given many excursions are on water, a basic level of water confidence is required also. With several bike rides of various lengths, 3-4 paddling opportunities, and several hikes, this trip helps you to “Get out there!” It’s the best way to experience rainforests, mountains, lakes, oceans and wetlands. You’ll experience some Maori culture, and the famous geothermal region of New Zealand too. While we do hit some of the iconic popular spots around New Zealand, we also take you off the beaten track to remote and wilderness locations.

    Is this trip right for you?

    If you:

    • love active holidays with opportunities for hiking, biking, or kayaking almost every day
    • enjoy stepping beyond your comfort zone with a unique activity like black water rafting
    • want to be outside in nature daily
    • enjoy being unplugged
    • enjoy meeting locals and staying in their homes
    • want to be inspired by local conservationists and environmentalists
    • want to support locally owned and operated businesses
    • value participating in activities that give back to the environment
    • and love to share all these experiences with other LGBT or LGBT friendly travellers

    ...then this trip is for you!
