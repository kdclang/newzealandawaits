Tours

We are delighted to offer four specially designed theme tours for spring 2016.

If you:

  • want to fully immerse yourself in the natural environment of your travel destination, spend time with locals, and learn about the land, the people, and the traditions of the culture
  • seek travel experiences that deepen your understanding of our connections globally, locally, and to the natural world
  • value contributing to local businesses and communities by choosing to be part of a sustainable travel experience
  • appreciate sharing these experiences with, as well as adding positively to, the experience of other like-minded, thoughtful travellers
  • enjoy establishing new life long friendships with other travellers

...then check out our trips below!

We know from our own travels that spending time with like-minded travellers is an amazing way to meet new people and develop life long friendships. We also know that at times we enjoy having the freedom and flexibility to wander off on our own and explore a different restaurant now and then. Our combination of group meals and meals on your own provides that balance.
