Arts and Culture

Major public galleries, local artists’ studios, cultural, historic, and contemporary museums, heritage attractions, theatre and performing arts… all these await the more artistically or culturally inclined. New Zealand has many thriving artist communities as well. Many have options of creating your own craft, perhaps a Maori bone carving.
