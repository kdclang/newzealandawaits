Some of our FAVORITE water activities include:

  • An overnight boat trip in a remote fiord, fishing for your evening meal, penguin viewing, all while listening to the exquisite sounds of the wilderness.
  • Kayaking to pristine, golden beaches accessed only by boat or kayak, and seal-spotting along the way. You may even be lucky enough to see a mother seal and pup!
  • Whale-watching or swimming with dolphins in gorgeous turquoise oceans.
  • Lake eco-tours to hot water beaches and natural hot pools.
